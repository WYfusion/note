## 后验分布

$$q(x_{t-1}|x_t, x_0) = \mathcal{N}(x_{t-1}; \tilde{\mu}_t, \tilde{\beta}_t \mathbf{I})$$

$$\tilde{\mu}_t = \frac{\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}\beta_t}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t}x_0 + \frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}(1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1})}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t}x_t$$

## ELBO

$$\log p(x_0) \geq \mathbb{E}_q\left[-\log\frac{q(x_{1:T}|x_0)}{p_\theta(x_{0:T})}\right]$$

简化后等价于：

$$\mathcal{L} = \sum_t \mathbb{E}\left[\|\epsilon - \epsilon_\theta(x_t, t)\|^2\right]$$

> [!important]
> 
> **ELBO 简化的关键**：Ho et al. 发现去掉 ELBO 中的权重项（unweighted loss）训练效果反而更好——这使得每个时间步的贡献相等，避免了对特定时间步的过拟合。
