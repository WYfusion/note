## 核心公式

### Gumbel Softmax

$$\hat{e}_t = \sum_{k=1}^{K} \frac{\exp((\log \pi_{\theta,k} + g_k) / \tau)}{\sum_j \exp((\log \pi_{\theta,j} + g_j) / \tau)} \cdot e_k$$

$g_k sim text{Gumbel}(0,1)$，$tau$ 为温度参数。

### Token2Text 奖励

$$R_{\text{ASR}}(\hat{\mathbf{e}}) = -\text{CER}(\text{Token2Text}(\hat{\mathbf{e}}), \text{text}_{\text{ref}})$$

### Token 级 KL

$$D_{\text{KL}}^{\text{token}} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} D_{\text{KL}}(\pi_\theta(\cdot | s_{<t}) \| \pi_{\text{ref}}(\cdot | s_{<t}))$$

### 总目标

$$\mathcal{L}_{\text{DiffRO}} = -\sum_i \lambda_i R_i(\hat{\mathbf{e}}) + \beta \cdot D_{\text{KL}}^{\text{token}}$$

## 与 RLHF / DPO 对比

|**维度**|**RLHF (PPO)**|**DPO**|**DiffRO**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**奖励来源**|Reward Model|隐式（偏好对）|**Token2Text + MTR**|
|**可微分性**|❌ 需 PPO|✅ 隐式|**✅ Gumbel Softmax**|
|**KL 约束**|序列级|隐式|**Token 级**|
|**多维奖励**|通常单一|单一|**✅ ASR/SER/MOS/AED**|
|**计算效率**|低|中|**高（无需合成语音）**|
